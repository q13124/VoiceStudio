## 📈 Quality Metrics Framework

### Metrics Implemented
- ✅ **MOS Score** - Mean Opinion Score (1.0-5.0)
- ✅ **Similarity** - Voice similarity (0.0-1.0)
- ✅ **Naturalness** - Prosody metrics (0.0-1.0)
- ✅ **SNR** - Signal-to-noise ratio (dB)
- ✅ **Artifacts** - Detection and scoring

### Quality Standards
- **Professional:** MOS ≥ 4.0, Similarity ≥ 0.85, Naturalness ≥ 0.80
- **High:** MOS ≥ 4.5, Similarity ≥ 0.90, Naturalness ≥ 0.85
- **Ultra:** MOS ≥ 4.8, Similarity ≥ 0.95, Naturalness ≥ 0.90
